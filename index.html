How to use this app


📋 The List

Shows all stores. Use the filter buttons at the top to show only HUMANA stores, only KG stores, stores you've visited, or ones you haven't been to yet. Scroll the filters sideways if you can't see them all.

🗺️ The Map

Shows all store locations as pins. Red pins = HUMANA. Blue pins = others. A ✓ on a pin means you've visited. Tap any pin to see a summary, then tap "View Details".

Tap the 📍 button (bottom-right) to turn your location on — you'll appear as a blue dot that follows you as you move. The button turns green while it's on; tap it again to turn location off. Your browser will ask for permission the first time.

📦 Inventory Tracker

Inside each store, tap "Set Last Delivery Date" to record when new stock arrived. The app will:

  • Count how many days have passed
  • Show a progress bar through the cycle
  • Estimate the current discount % (or exact KG price for weight stores)

Green = fresh stock, high prices. Orange = mid-cycle. Red = late cycle, best deals!

✅ Visited

Tap the big green "Mark as Visited" button inside any store. It turns into a checkmark and the card gets a green border in the list.

⚖️ KG vs Itemized

By KG stores charge by weight — you fill a bag and pay per kilogram. Prices drop every day. Itemized stores price each item individually.

🤝 Share & Contribute

Tap the 🤝 button in the top-right to share the stores you've added or edited. Copy share link gives you a link — anyone who opens it gets your stores merged onto their map. You can also download a file, or paste in a link/file someone sent you to import theirs. Duplicates are skipped automatically.

To help everyone, tap 🌍 Contribute to the official map — it opens a pre-filled GitHub form with your additions and corrections so they can be merged into the map that ships with the app.

📤 Maps.me Integration

Tap the 📤 button in the top-right to download a KML file with all store locations. In Maps.me: tap ☰ → Bookmarks → ⋮ → Import → select the file. All stores appear as bookmarks. Inside any store, tap 📍 Maps.me to jump straight to that store on the Maps.me map. To add a new store you discover, long-press on the spot in Maps.me to drop a pin, then bookmark it.
